Our product — MycoBlocks

MycoBlocks are lightweight, strong, and self-growing structural elements derived from fungal mycelium and regolith-based substrates. They can be grown on-site in space habitats using minimal energy and no heavy industrial machinery.

This drastically reduces launch costs and enables scalable construction for habitats, radiation shielding, and infrastructure.
