About us

Mycogalactica is a student-founded aerospace sustainability venture focused on enabling long-term human presence in space. We develop biologically-derived construction materials that reduce launch mass, cost, and environmental impact.

Our flagship product, MycoBlocks, uses engineered mycelium composites that can be grown in-situ using minimal inputs. This allows space infrastructure to be produced locally rather than transported from Earth.
