About Us

Mycogalactica is an early-stage aerospace materials company focused on enabling sustainable construction beyond Earth. We develop biologically grown building materials that drastically reduce the cost, mass, and environmental impact of space infrastructure.

Our work sits at the intersection of biotechnology, materials science, and aerospace engineering. We believe that humanity’s expansion into space must be done responsibly — with systems that are regenerative, lightweight, and resource-efficient.

Instead of launching every kilogram of building material from Earth, we design systems that grow structural components in situ using minimal inputs. This approach allows future missions to build habitats, research stations, and industrial platforms with far lower logistical and financial barriers.

Our Mission

To make off-world construction sustainable, scalable, and economically viable — enabling long-term human presence beyond Earth while minimizing environmental impact on both Earth and space environments.

Our Vision

A future where space infrastructure is not shipped, but grown. Where habitats, research facilities, and industrial structures are produced locally from biological systems, enabling resilient and sustainable expansion into space.
